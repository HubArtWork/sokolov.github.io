What is PractiThink?

We use different bots and algorithms to create a personalized study plan for a student based on their current level and progress. It’s totally up to the account owner to adopt our suggestions, partially use them or completely ignore them.

So you might think of it as a pacifist A.I.

PractiThink challenges students with personalized tests that will test their knowledge. As they work their way through the system, our algorithm will suggest a variety of content through the personalized study plan to make sure they’re always working on exactly that which they need the most help.
