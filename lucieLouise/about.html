Our idea

We had the idea to bring something that every family of our modern time will need and love: beauty, freedom, amusement, and hobbies at the same place for our kids. All of this at one fix price per month.

There is a passion for all types of experiences. We cut newborns hair no matter how old they are, celebrate birthday parties, organization of thematic evening, rental of our game room for your private parties for children (on demand) etc...The knowledge that we have is priceless and the concept is unique!
