Just some of Uppointment invoicing options

Auto-reminders and notifications

Set up invoice reminders to automatically remind your customers when payment becomes due. Get notified when an invoice gets paid and your customer will get a receipt in email - automatically.

Accept payments online

Offer customers more ways to pay to get paid faster. Use your own merchant account or accept credit cards and bank payments through Uppointment for as littleas 1.5% per transaction. Integrated with Stripe, PayPal, Square, Authorize.net and Braintree.

Recurring and scheduled billing

Improve your cash flow, set up automatic sending of recurring invoices or credit card payments for your loyal customers. Invoice is ready but not due yet, easily scheduled when you need it to be out and rest assured your customer got it then.

Miss-nothing invoicing

Easily add charges to the customer's invoice as they incur or at once. Invoices automatically saved to your Draft folder. When ready, send professional invoices to your customers in one click. We will send reminders if customers missed the due date.

Easy to share invoices

Deliverability is the most important feature of invoicing and at Uppointment we take it seriously. Sent to email by default, you will see clearly when email was delivered and open. Customers don’t check email? Send text to their phone or send a link to whatsapp or messenger.

Keeping good records - done, effortlessly

Every step of your invoicing and payment activity automatically syncs with customers' records, products or services settings and your income stats in your Uppointment account
